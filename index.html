Biography

I am a Postdoctoral researcher working at the intersection of physical optics and image/signal processing, with a focus of understanding the bottleneck problems in biomedical sciences, which can be solved with quickly evolving optical and computational techniques. I am highly motivated for developing a long term academic research career in “unconventional optical systems” and “light transport and associated inverse problems”.
